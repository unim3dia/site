AI Studies
An experimental image-and-motion practice focused on character, worldbuilding, and visual tone. This page works like a sandbox: pull elements around, open pieces up close, and scan the range of visual directions.
01
Character Systems
Eggs, manboys, bullies
Recurring personas built through prompt iteration, selection, and visual editing. The aim is not novelty alone, but recognizable characters with their own emotional logic.
02
Worldbuilding
Deserts, cities, abstractions
Environments developed as mood frameworks: surreal deserts, stressed urban surfaces, broken interiors, and places that feel filmable even when they are invented.
03
Portrait Direction
Faces, types, archetypes
Portraits used to test casting, texture, lighting, and era. These studies are about point of view as much as realism.
