About

I direct, edit, and color grade video, and these days, prompt and generate it. My work is characterized by systematic thinking about storytelling executed across wildly different mediums and scales, from billion-view children's content to meditative Super 16mm cinema to experimental digital art. Feature Documentary · Super 16mm Film

Play Trailer

Deep in the Florida Everglades, time moves differently — measured in the passage of light through cypress canopy and the patient drift of water through sawgrass prairie.

Shot entirely on 16mm film, this experimental documentary explores the ecological texture of one of North America's most endangered landscapes. The camera becomes a witness, not a narrator.
