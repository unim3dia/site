Meditative Cinema

Shot on Super 16mm.
No narration.
Just the Everglades, breathing.

Feature Documentary · Super 16mm Film

Play Trailer

Deep in the Florida Everglades, time moves differently — measured not in hours but in the slow passage of light through cypress canopy and the patient drift of water through sawgrass prairie.

Shot entirely on 16mm film, this experimental documentary explores the ecological and spiritual texture of one of North America's most endangered landscapes. The camera becomes a witness, not a narrator.
